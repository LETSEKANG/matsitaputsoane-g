About Us

Miss Putsoane Supermarket is a family-owned business that has been serving the community for over a decade. We take pride in offering a wide range of products, from fresh produce to household essentials, at affordable prices. Our team is made up of dedicated individuals who are passionate about providing excellent customer service. We believe in building strong relationships with our customers and are committed to meeting their needs. At Miss Putsoane Supermarket, we strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment for both our customers and employees. We are proud to be a part of the community and look forward to serving you for many years to come.
